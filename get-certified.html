Who needs a Safe Place to Visit certification?

Any social establishment that wants to

  • ensure the safety of their patrons' health and well-being
  • get recognized for their efforts toward consumer safety and security

Auditing Parameters

Based on the industry requirements, our experts have curated a set of distinct parameters that will help test and establish safety and hygiene standards in the social establishments. Below mentioned are the Safe Place to Visit parameters that will give you an indication of the specified framework.

Premise Safety & Hygiene

Food Safety

Environment Safety

Infrastructural Facilities

Physical Security

Electrical Safety

Fire Safety

Personal Hygiene

How to Get Certified?

You can become a Safe Place to Visit by simply following these steps. So, get started with your Safety and Hygiene Audit to become a certified Safe Place to Visit.

Registering under the program

The step includes enrolling your Facility under the ‘Safe Place to Stay’ Initiative by filling the form in our registration section.

Stage 1 Audit

The stage 1 audit will help you understand your level of compliance with the standard.

Stage 2 Audit (Final Audit)

This will be the final assessment of your adherence to the standard. If you score 70% and above you will be certified as “Safe Place to Stay.”

Issuing of Certificate

If you qualify as Safe Place to Stay, you will be issued a certificate and usage guidelines.

*After the stage 1 audit, you have 30 days to make the required changes before opting for the stage 2 audit. If you are confident about the compliance standards (after reviewing the standard), you can also skip the stage 1 audit and opt directly for the stage 2 audit.
